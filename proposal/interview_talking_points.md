# Interview Talking Points

## Opening

This project is a first-model build, so I would optimize for a model that is robust, interpretable and easy to calibrate rather than overly complex.

## What I would check first

1. Weekly date alignment between Google, Meta and call tracking.
2. Call definitions: raw calls vs qualified calls vs booked appointments.
3. Tracking breaks or attribution-window changes.
4. Channel correlation.
5. Available calibration signals.
6. Operational constraints such as clinic capacity.

## Why priors matter

With 18-24 months of weekly data, correlated channels and limited experiments, priors are not decoration. They help prevent implausible ROI assignments.

## Why 2-week MVP plus 4-week final delivery

The first model can be built quickly if data access is ready. But the value comes from calibration, validation and making budget recommendations defensible.
